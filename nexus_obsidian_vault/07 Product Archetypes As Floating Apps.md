# 07 Product Archetypes As Floating Apps

## 目的

定義未來大型產品如何作為 workspace floating app 接入 NEXUS。

## Airtasker-like Floating App

需要能力：

- marketplace
- task feed
- profiles
- attachments
- comments
- task chat
- offers
- payments
- reviews
- moderation

## Reddit-like Floating App

需要能力：

- feed
- thread
- comments
- reactions
- profiles
- moderation
- search
- save to notes

## Instagram-like Floating App

需要能力：

- feed
- media upload
- profiles
- reactions
- comments
- follow graph
- notifications

## Canva-like Floating App

需要能力：

- canvas
- asset library
- templates
- layers
- export
- collaboration
- comments
- AI assist

## 原則

這些都不是外部網站，也不是新 route 為主。

它們應該是 NEXUS workspace floating apps。
